About Eventify

What is Eventify?

Eventify is a platform where students and users can explore, create, and share events happening around their community. Whether you want to find new activities, share your own event, or connect with others, Eventify makes it easy and fun.

Create and Share

Users can register and log in to create new events by filling out a simple form. Upload images, set event details like date and location, and share it with everyone on the platform. All events are publicly listed for others to discover.

Join Our Community

Eventify is more than just a website — it’s a community hub for students and event enthusiasts. Stay updated, meet new people, and make the most of every event.
